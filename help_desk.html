Frequently Asked Questions Q&A


Q: If the information provided on the FIFA System is wrong, how can I give feedback?

You can declare to us through the "Suggestion" function, or you can get in touch with us through the contact information in "About Us" .


Q: After I submit a suggestion, how will I get feedback on the suggestion?

The system will send relevant feedback to the email address you provided.
